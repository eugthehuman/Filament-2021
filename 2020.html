About

Filament is the annual showcase of short films produced by the graduating Broadcast & Cinema Studies students of Nanyang Technological University’s Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information (WKWSCI).

2020 marks the 14th edition of Filament. In these extraordinary times, Filament goes online for the first time, streaming our students' works to our audience in the comfort of their homes.

A total of 9 shorts—6 documentaries and 3 fiction narratives—are presented this year. Each film is not just the fruit of a year-long journey; it is also a promising step into the world of professional filmmaking as students conclude their training and education at WKWSCI.

Baby Boy

Documentary

Length: 24 minutes 59 seconds

In this coming-of-age film, 17-year-old Zaki’s ambitions are disrupted by his troubled family past, and he now has to wrestle with the idea of fatherhood when he realises that his ex-girlfriend is pregnant.

Director - Jessica Heng
Producers - Bambby Cheuk, Toby Wu
Director of Photography - Chua Zong Xuan

Beyond Words

Documentary

Length: 19 minutes 28 seconds

16-year-old Amanda leaves the mainstream education system to care for her younger sister with a life-threatening rare condition, as her family bands together to support the caregiving process and advocate for inclusion.

Director - Amanda Chang
Producer - Esther Rim
Director of Photography - Chiara So
Sound - Julia Ang

Bhanja

Documentary

Length: 15 minutes 45 seconds

As a child of a Gurkha, Shuvod Rai has spent 20 years of his life growing up in Singapore. In light of his father’s retirement from the service, Shuvod returns to Nepal to assist with his parents move back to the South Asian country. However, while Shuvod grew up in Singapore and built a life for himself in the city-state, he might not be able to return. Amidst the uncertainties, Shuvod faces the question behind the notion of ‘home’. Where exactly is home to him? Is home the country he grew up in or the country reflected on his passport?

Director - Au Yeong Hui Wen
Producer - Cheong Wan Ting
Director of Photography - Vanessa Cheng You Ching
Editor/Sound - Wang Xin Ning Cherilyn

Immobile

Documentary

Length: 16 minutes 37 seconds

Following the implementation of the Personal Mobility Device (PMD) ban in late 2019, Raihan, a full time food delivery rider struggles to cope with the implications of the ban — all while trying to balance between family and making ends meet.

Director - Eugene Tang
Producer - Charlene Chua
Assistant Director - Jeremy Teo
Director of Photography - Lo Hoi Ying

More With Less

Documentary

Length: 15 minutes 36 seconds

Minimalism is often associated with words like ‘boring’ and ‘deprivation’, where it is pegged with negative connotations to the uninitiated. More with Less challenges this notion by exploring the benefits that minimalism brings to those who adopt it, and the positive impact it has on the environment. Through the lives of Jane, a pastry chef, and Amanda, a KonMari consultant-in-training, the film reveals an unknown and misunderstood part of this lifestyle choice.

Director - Kevyn Puah
Producer - Amanda Jane Lim
Assistant Director/Sound - Aronn Lim
Director of Photography/Editor - Gina Peh

Nice To Meat You

Narrative

Length: 15 minutes 3 seconds

Jeffery Tan has always craved human flesh, but his father has always been around to help him cope with his urges. Since his passing, Jeffery opens his home to a new tenant - Renae. As he embarks on a plan to eat her, Jeffery gets torn between his unsatiated desire for human flesh and his growing feelings for his new roommate.

Producer - Brenlyn Ng
Director - Nicholas Teo
Director of Photography - Darrell Er
Art Director - Natasha Wee
Editors - Natasha Wee and Nicholas Teo

Our Friend Was Killed By The Hotdog Man

Narrative

Length: 9 minutes 22 seconds

In a land abandoned by space and time, duo – Remy and Sanjay seek to avenge the death of their friend who died a victim of the evil Hotdog Man. They encounter Kong Lee who leads them on a quest to defeat the Hotdog Man.

Director - Eunice Loh
Producer - Clara Lee Wa
Director of Photography/Editor - Jonan Liang
Assistant Producer - Park Yibin

The Stray Moth

Narrative

Length: 14 minutes 21 seconds

As Kian mourns the passing of his late wife, Ping, during the Hungry Ghost Festival, he encounters The Stranger - an unsettling, yet familiar woman. The Stranger introduces the eclectic world of Getai to Kian, where he is forced to face his true identity.

Director - Barnabas Chua
Producer - Megan Tan
Scriptwriter - Chua Shu Ern
Co-Producer - Kenneth Huang

The Red Dustbin

Documentary

Length: 14 minutes 08 seconds

The Red Dustbin provides a window into the relationship between government efforts and individual responsibilities. In 2016, a novel solution was implemented - food waste is thrown into red dustbins, then emptied into an onsite food waste digester to be recycled. Today, only three stalls regularly use the red dustbins. So, what’s stopping more stall owners from contributing to the red dustbins?

Director - Hannah Tay
Producer - Vess Chua
Director of Photography - Nursyakir (Syark) Taher
Sound Mixologist - Lee Yi Hong
